Overview

Welcome to the Renert School celebration of learning challenge 2020. Through this challenge your mathematical skills will be put to the test. Problems contained in the maze cover topics from kindergarten mathematics to advanced topics in calculus, probability, number theory, and more. How far will you get?

While you are working on this challenge, you will encounter other Google Forms — you do not need to press “submit” on those forms (doing so may navigate away from the page that tells you where to go next), until you get to Level 42.

Submission details

To submit how far you make it through the maze, please fill out this form: https://tinyurl.com/2020mathchallengeform. You do not need to submit at every level, only the highest level you achieve. You may submit as many times as you wish.
